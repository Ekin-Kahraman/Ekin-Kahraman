### Projects

**[bulk-rnaseq-differential-expression](https://github.com/Ekin-Kahraman/bulk-rnaseq-differential-expression)** — R/DESeq2 pipeline on GSE152075 (n=484 SARS-CoV-2 swabs). 1,902 DE genes, ISG pathway enrichment, sex-interaction models uncovering 12 genes with sex-biased immune response, viral load stratification. CI/CD, renv, Zenodo DOI.

**[single-cell-rnaseq-immune-profiling](https://github.com/Ekin-Kahraman/single-cell-rnaseq-immune-profiling)** — Python/scanpy pipeline for PBMC cell type profiling (2,638 cells, 6 immune cell types). Automated marker-based annotation, multi-resolution Leiden clustering. CI on Python 3.10–3.12, pytest.

**[safetynett](https://github.com/Ekin-Kahraman/safetynett)** — AI-powered clinical safety netting for NHS primary care. Automated patient follow-up across 40+ conditions, red flag detection from NICE CKS, GP escalation. Built in 2.5 hours at the [OpenClaw Clinical Hackathon](https://clinicalhackathon.com). [Live demo](https://safetynett.lovable.app).
